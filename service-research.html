Advanced Materials Characterization

Systematic investigation of materials properties using state-of-the-art characterization techniques, with emphasis on electrical properties under extreme conditions.

  • Temperature Dependencies: Systematic study of superconducting transitions from 4K to room temperature.

  • Annealing Optimization: Development of precise heat treatment protocols for materials property recovery.
