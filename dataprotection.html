General Information

This data protection declaration informs you about the type, scope and purpose of the processing of personal data on this website. Personal data is all data with which you can be personally identified.

Privacy-friendly

This website does not use cookies and collects minimal personal data.

Contact and Communication

Maximum Data Protection

This website deliberately uses no contact form to minimize the processing of personal data and maximize your data protection.

Direct Email Communication

For contact, please use direct email communication:

  • Email address: website@daniel-hackl.com
  • No server-side data processing by us
  • No storage of form data on our servers

Data Processing During Email Contact

When you contact us by email, your data is only processed by the email providers involved:

  • Your email provider (e.g. Gmail, Outlook, etc.)
  • My email provider for the response

Legal Basis and Storage Duration

Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest)
Our legitimate interest lies in answering your inquiry. Emails are only stored for as long as necessary to process your inquiry.

Hosting and Technical Processing

Important Notice Regarding USA Hosting

This website is hosted on servers in the USA. This has specific data protection implications that you should be aware of.

Server Log Files

When visiting this website, the following information is automatically collected and stored in the USA:

  • IP address of the accessing computer
  • Date and time of access
  • Name and URL of the retrieved file
  • Website from which access was made (referrer URL)
  • Browser used and operating system if applicable
  • Operating system and language used

Legal Particularities of USA Hosting

Third Country Transfer

Since the servers are located in the USA, your data is transferred to a third country for which there is no adequacy decision by the EU Commission. US authorities may demand access to data under certain circumstances (CLOUD Act, FISA 702).

Additional Data Protection Measures

Technical Protection Measures
  • Daily log rotation to minimize data storage
  • Web statistics completely disabled
  • No external tracking services or CDNs
  • Local delivery of all assets (CSS, JavaScript, fonts)
