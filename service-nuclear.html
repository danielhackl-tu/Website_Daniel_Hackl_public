Nuclear Safety & Radiation Protection

Comprehensive radiation protection consulting and nuclear safety analysis

Service Overview

With extensive experience in radiation protection and nuclear safety, I provide comprehensive consulting services for nuclear facilities, research institutions, and regulatory bodies. My expertise covers dose rate calculations, neutron flux analysis, and development of radiation protection concepts.

Key Services

Radiation Protection Concepts

Development of comprehensive radiation protection strategies and safety protocols for nuclear facilities and research environments.

Dose Rate Calculations

Precise calculation of dose rates and radiation exposure levels using advanced computational methods and measurement techniques.

Neutron Flux Analysis

Determination of thermal and fast neutron flux in reactor cores and irradiation positions using neutron activation analysis.

Safety Documentation

Preparation of safety reports, regulatory compliance documents, and technical assessments for nuclear projects.
