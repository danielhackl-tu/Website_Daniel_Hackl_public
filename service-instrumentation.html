Neutron Spin Measurement Systems

Design and implementation of sophisticated measurement systems for research and industrial applications, with emphasis on precision and reliability.

  • Four-Point Probe Systems: Custom electrical measurement setups for precise resistivity determination with automated data acquisition.
  • Cryogenic Measurements: Integration with 17T superconducting magnet systems for ultra-low temperature characterization (4 K - 300 K).
  • Multi-Parameter Monitoring: Simultaneous measurement of electrical, thermal, and environmental parameters with synchronized data logging.
  • High-Precision Sensors: Integration of various sensor types including thermoluminescence dosimeters and gamma spectrometers.

Custom Instrumentation Design

Mechanical design and fabrication of specialized measurement fixtures and instrumentation for extreme environments.

  • CAD Design: Proficient in Autodesk Inventor for designing custom sample holders, measurement fixtures, and experimental apparatus.
  • In-Situ Measurements: Development of instrumentation for real-time monitoring during materials processing and thermal treatments.
  • Extreme Environment Design: Experience with vacuum-compatible, cryogenic, and radiation-resistant instrumentation design.
  • Manufacturing Integration: Design for manufacturability considering tolerances, assembly procedures, and maintenance requirements.

Control Systems & Automation

Development of automated control systems and data acquisition platforms for complex experimental setups.

  • Python-Based Control: Custom software development for instrument control, data acquisition, and real-time system monitoring.
  • Temperature Control Integration: Automated thermal cycling and annealing processes with precise temperature profiles and safety interlocks.
  • Data Synchronization: Multi-channel data acquisition with timestamp correlation and automated data validation procedures.
  • User Interface Development: Intuitive control interfaces with real-time visualization and alarm management systems.
